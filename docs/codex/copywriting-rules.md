# Copywriting Rules

## Preferred Style
- Simple
- Calm
- Concrete
- Product-specific
- Emotionally warm without hype

## Avoid Phrases
- "Boost productivity"
- "Achieve more"
- "Crush goals"
- "Track everything"
- "Become your best self overnight"
- Childish XP/game language

## Preferred Concepts
- Constancia
- Progreso visual
- Pequeños avances
- Hábitos sostenibles
- Motivación calmada
- Familias de hábitos
- Claridad
- Ritmo

## Legal Copy Warning
- Do not casually change privacy/terms content.
- Keep legal text accurate to current website and app behavior.

## SEO Copy Rules
- Avoid keyword stuffing.
- Keep metadata honest and concise.
