# Rutio Brand Reference

## Product Definition
- Rutio is a calm, premium habit tracker.
- Current web goal: explain the product clearly and collect beta/waitlist signups.

## Product Pillars
- Sustainable habits
- Visual progress
- Habit families
- Diary/tasks integration
- XP, achievements, and calm motivation

## Tone Rules
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Emotionally motivating
- Clear
- Not childish
- Not aggressive productivity

## Language To Avoid
- Childish game language
- Fake urgency
- "Crush your goals"
- Generic SaaS/productivity buzzwords
- Unsupported claims

## Visual Direction
- Clean layouts
- Soft gradients
- Rounded cards
- Generous spacing
- Premium app landing feel
- iOS-first sensibility

## Product Truth Guardrails
- Do not invent features.
- Do not fake screenshots.
- Do not fake metrics.
- Keep beta/waitlist status honest.
