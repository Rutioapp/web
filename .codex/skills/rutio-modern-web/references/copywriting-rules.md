# Copywriting Rules

## Preferred Style
- Simple, calm, and concrete.
- Product-specific over generic productivity copy.
- Emotionally warm without hype.

## Avoid
- "Boost productivity"
- "Achieve more"
- "Crush goals"
- "Track everything"
- Overnight transformation promises
- Childish XP/game-heavy wording

## Preferred Concepts
- Consistency
- Visual progress
- Small steady improvements
- Sustainable habits
- Calm motivation
- Habit families
- Clarity and rhythm

## CTA And SEO
- Keep CTA language honest and low-pressure.
- Keep metadata concise and truthful.
- Avoid keyword stuffing or exaggerated claims.

## Legal Safety
- Do not casually edit privacy/terms text.
- Keep all policy statements aligned with real app/web behavior.
