# Rutio Brand Reference

## Product Definition
- Rutio is a calm, premium habit tracker.
- Landing page goal: explain the product clearly and capture beta/waitlist signups.

## Product Pillars
- Sustainable habits
- Visual progress
- Habit families
- Diary/tasks integration
- XP, achievements, and calm motivation

## Tone Direction
- Calm, trustworthy, emotionally motivating, clear.
- Product-specific language over generic productivity slogans.

## Language To Avoid
- Childish game-like phrasing.
- Fake urgency or hype.
- Aggressive lines like "crush your goals."
- Unsupported claims.

## Visual Direction
- Clean layouts with intentional spacing.
- Soft gradients and rounded cards.
- Premium app-landing feel with balanced hierarchy.

## Truth Guardrails
- Do not invent features.
- Do not fake screenshots, metrics, or social proof.
- Keep beta/waitlist status honest.
